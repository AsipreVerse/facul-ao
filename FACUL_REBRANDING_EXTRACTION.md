## Part 2: Extracted Requirements

### 2.1 Brand Architecture

| Brand Entity | Purpose | Visual Treatment |
|--------------|---------|------------------|
| **GRUPO FACUL** | Parent/holding company | Dark navy blue (#192E4D), bold typography, "F" icon in ellipse |
| **FACUL Centro Académico Digital** | Educational/training subsidiary | Lighter blue palette, dynamic "swoosh" graphic element over 'A' and 'C' |

### 2.2 Logo Concepts

#### GRUPO FACUL (Corporate Parent)
- **Concept:** Solidez, Tradição e Liderança Corporativa (Solidity, Tradition, Corporate Leadership)
- **Colour:** Dark navy blue (#192E4D)
- **Typography:** Bold, uppercase "FACUL" with "GRUPO" in grey (neutral, sophisticated)
- **Icon:** "F" lettermark inside ellipse
- **Differentiator:** Simplicity and dark colour are the main differentiators
- **Purpose:** Anchor brand representing the consolidated, reliable, strong parent company
- **Positioning:** Symbol of the organisational backbone, communicating security, experience, authority and permanence

#### FACUL Centro Académico Digital (Education)
- **Concept:** Inovação, Conhecimento e Crescimento Digital (Innovation, Knowledge, Digital Growth)
- **Colour:** Softer blue (more accessible and friendly for educational environment)
- **Graphic Element:** "Swoosh" or curved line over letters 'A' and 'C' suggesting movement, progress, learning pathway, digital data flow
- **Differentiator:** Light blue colour and dynamic graphic element transform the brand to be younger, agile, future-oriented
- **Positioning:** Innovative educational face of the group; guide on the learning journey using technology as the primary tool
