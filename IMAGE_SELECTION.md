# FACUL Image & Logo Selection

## Logo Variants Identified

Based on extraction from FACUL REBRANDING - 2026.pdf:

### 1. GRUPO FACUL (Corporate Parent)
- **Icon:** "F" lettermark inside ellipse (oval ring)
- **Text:** "FACUL" in bold uppercase + "GRUPO" below in grey
- **Colour:** Dark Navy (#192E4D)
- **Usage:** Corporate parent branding, boardroom signage, formal communications
- **Visible in:** facul_rebrand-012.jpg (wayfinding), facul_rebrand-013.jpg (wall plaque), facul_rebrand-014.jpg (boardroom 3D)

### 2. FACUL Centro Académico Digital (Education)
- **Icon:** "F" lettermark inside ellipse with dynamic "swoosh" curve over letters
- **Text:** "FACUL" + "CENTRO ACADÉMICO DIGITAL" subtitle
- **Colour:** Lighter blue palette (#447AAE, #1B476B)
- **Usage:** Educational platform, training materials, digital communications
- **Visible in:** facul_rebrand-003.jpg, facul_rebrand-004.jpg, facul_rebrand-005.jpg, facul_rebrand-006.jpg, facul_rebrand-011.jpg, facul_rebrand-015.jpg, facul_rebrand-018.jpg

### 3. 3D Glass Logo Mark
- **Description:** Crystalline/glass render of "F" inside ellipse ring
- **Usage:** Hero visual, premium brand asset
- **Visible in:** facul_rebrand-001.jpg, facul_rebrand-009.jpg
